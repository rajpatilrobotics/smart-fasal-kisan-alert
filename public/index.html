Core features for the hackathon problem statement

01

Smart crop recommendation

Suggests crops using soil type, rainfall pattern, groundwater depth, crop water need and local risk.

02

Dry spell alerts

Flags irrigation stress using localized weather, moisture and crop stage inputs.

03

Voice and SMS advisory

Designed for low literacy and low connectivity farmers through Indic language messages and calls.

04

Crop health logging

Farmers can report crop problems by photo, symptom text or voice for AI triage.

05

RSK expert follow up

High risk crop cases are routed to Rythu Seva Kendra experts with AI summaries.

06

District dashboard

MP office and district teams can view village risk, crop stress and pending expert cases.
